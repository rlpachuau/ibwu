From President

Greetings in the matchless name of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ,

Dear Sisters and Brothers in Christ, on behalf of the India Baptist Women’s Union I would like to express my heartfelt greetings to you all especially in these difficult times.

By the Grace of the Almighty, I am glad for this opportunity to connect with you all through this editorial. It is by His Grace that we are able to collectively serve in the kingdom of Our Lord.

It gives me immense joy to share with you an additional platform for our organization in the form of a thoughtfully designed website which can be accessed globally

I had observed the outstanding work done by the previous committee members and now, I am excited to observe the excellence and enthusiasm of the present working committee in carrying forward the mission of our organization.

In these unprecedented times, our team has decided to focus on the following objectives:

To get more young women involved in our mission work To see significant growth in numbers within our member teams To improve the connectivity and communication between member teams To increase the interaction between the executive members and the member teams To promote the interests of the member teams in their organizations and churches

It is the need of the hour in these difficult times for all of us to work together for the common cause of our faith in Our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ

Philippines 4:13 teaches us that – I can do all things through Christ who strengthens me.

Let us learn from this scripture so that we might be fruitful where our Lord has places us to serve.

May the Grace of Our Lord Jesus Christ bring peace and blessings to all of us across the nations.

In His Service,

Mrs. Mercy Mayuri Moses
President – IBWU
