    Our Mission

    • To get more young women involved in our mission work
    • To see significant growth in numbers within our member
    • To improve the connectivity and communication between member
    • To increase the interaction between the executive members and the member
    • To promote the interests of the member teams in their organizations and churches

    Aims and Objective

    • To realize and give expression to the essential oneness in the Lord Jesus Christ of the Churches of Baptist faith and order.
    • To provide means for united consideration and solution of their common problems and for proclamation of their common principles while maintaining the spiritual unity and cooperation with all believers in the Lord Jesus Christ in common task committed to them.
    • To present churches of Baptist Faith and Order in all matters where united Action is called for.
    • To promote a spiritual fellowship and partnership among the members to strengthen their faith and witness.
    • To give expression to their point of view or moral and social issues both at national and international levels.
    • To nourish and deepen the spiritual life of persons, family and churches under their jurisdiction.
    • To promote the obligations of Christian stewardship among its member bodies.
